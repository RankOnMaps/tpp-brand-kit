10 · Rules

Hold the line.

Do

  • Default to white with ink type and hairline-bordered cards.
  • Forest anchors, green is the single action colour.
  • Frame every price as "from".
  • Set display in Inter Tight 900 all caps, tight tracking.
  • Run mono receipts and spec labels in corners.
  • Australian spelling for client-facing copy.

Don't

  • Don't flood the page with green, it only works when scarce.
  • Don't mix in the Rank On Maps palette or pin logo.
  • Don't show rep commission, margin or prestart credit. Ever.
  • Don't use sterile stock or skyline filler.
  • Don't use em-dashes, semicolons or AI-slop flourishes.
  • Don't set the editorial italic more than once per piece.
